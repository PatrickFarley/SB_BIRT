Secure Browser Functional Requirements

A. Security Related Requirements

  1. F01. The Secure browser shall NOT provide a means to clear browser cache.
  2. F02. The secure browser shall NOT provide a means to clear browser cookies.
  3. F03. The secure browser shall provide a means to clear system clipboard.
  4. F04. The secure browser shall provide a means to retrieve system MAC address.
  5. F05. The secure browser shall NOT provide a means to retrieve the system IP address(es).
  6. F06. The secure browser shall NOT provide a means to retrieve the current list of running processes, their PIDs and their PPIDs.
  7. F07. The secure browser shall provide a means to obtain the application start time.
  8. F08. The secure browser shall provide a means to query if Spaces is enabled or not, for macOS.
  9. F15. The secure browser shall provide a means to set permissive mode.
  10. F49. The secure browser shall provide a means to query the status of permissive mode.
  11. F31. The secure browser shall be able to determine whether blacklisted processes are running on the client machine.
  12. F36. The secure browser may be able to get device info (optional).
  13. F37. The secure browser shall be able to lock down the environment.
  14. F38. The secure browser shall be able to check if environment is secure for testing.
  15. F46. The secure browser shall be able to get the status of a particular browser capability.
  16. F47. The secure browser shall be able to set the status of a particular browser capability.

B. Device Related Requirements

  1. F09. The secure browser shall provide a means to get system volume.
  2. F10. The secure browser shall provide a means to set system volume.
  3. F11. The secure browser shall provide a means to mute system volume.
  4. F12. The secure browser shall provide a means to unmute system volume.
  5. F13. The secure browser shall provide a means to check if system volume is Muted.
  6. F14. The secure browser shall provide a means to close the browser.

C. Text-to-Speech (TTS) Related Requirements

  1. F16. The secure browser shall provide shall provide a means to play arbitrary text.
  2. F17. The secure browser shall provide a means to stop TTS speech in progress.
  3. F18. The secure browser shall provide a means to pause TTS speech in progress.
  4. F19. The secure browser shall provide a means to retrieve list of TTS voice packs available on the machine.
  5. F20. The secure browser shall provide a means to get the current TTS voice pack set as default on the machine.
  6. F21. The secure browser shall provide a means to resume previously paused TTS speech.
  7. F22. The secure browser shall provide a means events to notify the web application of status and progress of TTS.
  8. F23. The secure browser shall provide a means to set a TTS voice pack as the current voice.
  9. F28. The secure browser shall provide a means to get/set TTS pitch.
  10. F29. The secure browser shall provide a means to get/set TTS rate.
  11. F30. The secure browser shall provide a means to get/set TTS volume.

D. Audio Recording Related Requirements

These requirements are optional.

  1. F25. The secure browser may provide a means to start an audio recording.
  2. F26. The secure browser may provide a means to stop an audio recording.
  3. F27. The secure browser may provide a means to playback an audio recording.
  4. F40. The secure browser may provide a means to set audio recorder status.
  5. F41. The secure browser may provide a means to get audio recorder capabilities.
  6. F42. The secure browser may provide a means to retrieve a recording for playback.
  7. F43. The secure browser may provide a means to stop playback.
  8. F44. The secure browser may provide a means to pause playback.
  9. F45. The secure browser may provide a means to resume playback of a paused audio recording.
  10. F48. The secure browser may provide a means to list all available audio recordings.

E. Standards Compliance Requirements

  1. F32. The secure browser shall be HTML5 compliant.
  2. F34. The secure browser shall be CSS3 compliant.

F. Miscellaneous Requirements

  1. F35. The secure browser shall check for an appropriate Global Object for API use.
